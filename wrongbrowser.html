Classis

(Classis Student System)

Classis aims to be the ubiquitous classroom information assistant for schools. It is a complete student tracking, reporting and information management system.

Classis is designed for modern standards compliant browsers. If you don't have an up-to-date, xml-compliant browser then Classis will probably crash your browser, or at the very least Classis will neither look nor work correctly.

Classis is developed for, and works best with, Mozilla Firefox.
